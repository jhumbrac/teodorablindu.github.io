Hi! I'm Teodora Blindu, a graphic designer with a tendency of filling up my free time with side projects.

100 new ___ is a personal project I started to explore creativity in a way that works for me: doing a creative action every day for 100 days. I've found that the on and off intense 100 days routine has really worked for me. The idea of my 100 days experiment was inspired by Michael Bierut's workshop for the graduate graphic design students and Yale School of Art.

Here are some of the 100 days projects I have completed or am in the process of completing.

100 NEW PEOPLE

From February 2 to May 12, 2015 I met one new person every day and recorded the moment by taking a photo with them. Throughout the course of the project I traveled to 10 different cities on two continents, meeting a very interesting group of people that kept me inspired every day. You can see the people I have met and their stories here.

100 NEW EXPERIENCES
