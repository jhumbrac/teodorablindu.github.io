Current project: 100new Experiences, 27 Jun–04 Oct 2015

Day 26: Sebastian

27 February, 2015 in Brussels

26 years old and from Belgium but currently living in London

Speaks English and French

Describe the meeting circumstance

My friend introduced me to Sebastian. As we were sitting in a cafe waiting for him, the meeting was preplanned in a way so I wanted to try an experiment. I brainstormed different introductory first liners because often times we introduce ourselves with a hello, my name is, how are you and etc other common phrases. When Sebastian arrived, I was inspired by his jacket and introduced myself as "Hi, I'm Teodora. Nice jacket, can I have it?” He nervously laughed and said “Erm thanks, you can try it on I guess.” He proceeded to sit down, take off his jacket, place it on a chair and change the topic.

What I learned

First liners are important. After explaining to him my project, Sebastian admitted thinking that I was bizarre at first. Then he warmed up and was very nice, as noted in the photo when he even let me borrow the jacket for the photo.

Hi! I'm Teodora Blindu, a graphic designer with a tendency of filling up my free time with side projects.

100 new ___ is a personal project I started to explore creativity in a way that works for me: doing a creative action every day for 100 days. I've found that the on and off intense 100 days routine has really worked for me. The idea of my 100 days experiment was inspired by Michael Bierut's workshop for the graduate graphic design students and Yale School of Art.

Here are some of the 100 days projects I have completed or am in the process of completing.

100 NEW PEOPLE

From February 2 to May 12, 2015 I met one new person every day and recorded the moment by taking a photo with them. Throughout the course of the project I traveled to 10 different cities on two continents, meeting a very interesting group of people that kept me inspired every day.
